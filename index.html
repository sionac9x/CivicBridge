Why I Built CivicBridge

Too many students feel disconnected from the political process — not because they don't care, but because the process feels intimidating and inaccessible. I built CivicBridge to change that. This tool exists to lower the barrier between young people and their elected representatives, turning a confusing system into something anyone can navigate in minutes.

🎓

Education & Background

I'm a student passionate about the intersection of technology and civic life. My academic work focuses on political science, public policy, and how digital tools can strengthen democratic participation among young people.

Open States API Key

Required for live state representative data

To get accurate, real-time data on your state legislators and governor, CivicBridge uses the Open States API — a free civic data service.

  1. Visit open.pluralpolicy.com and create a free account
  2. Request an API key from your profile page
  3. Paste it below and click Save

Your key is stored only in your browser — never shared with anyone except Open States.
